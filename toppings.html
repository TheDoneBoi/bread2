The perfect companion

Best Bread Toppings

A slice of great bread deserves a great topping. From the beautifully simple to the extravagantly gourmet — here are our favorites.

Timeless Picks

Classic Toppings

The staples that have stood the test of time. Sometimes simplicity is perfection.

🧈

Salted Butter

The undisputed champion. Good European-style butter with visible salt crystals on warm bread is a spiritual experience. Use cultured butter for an extra tangy kick.

Essential
🍯

Honey

Raw, local honey drizzled over a thick slice of sourdough or challah — especially with butter underneath. Buckwheat honey adds a deep, molasses-like complexity.

Sweet
🫒

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

The Mediterranean way. Pour some grassy, peppery EVOO onto a saucer, add a splash of balsamic and flaky salt, and dip your bread. Heaven with ciabatta or focaccia.

Mediterranean
🍓

Jam & Preserves

From strawberry to apricot to fig — fruit preserves are bread's oldest sweet companion. Look for small-batch preserves with visible fruit chunks and less sugar.

Classic
🧀

Cream Cheese

Thick, cool cream cheese on a toasted bagel or rye bread. Go plain, or mix in chives, everything-bagel seasoning, or smoked salmon for the full New York experience.

New York
🥜

Peanut Butter

Creamy or crunchy — the great debate. Natural peanut butter on whole wheat toast is a protein-packed powerhouse. Add banana slices and honey for the ultimate combo.

Comfort Food
Elevated Pairings

Gourmet Toppings

For when you want to turn a simple slice into a culinary moment.

🥑

Smashed Avocado

The millennial classic. Ripe avocado smashed with lemon juice, red pepper flakes, and flaky Maldon salt on toasted sourdough. Add a poached egg to enter brunch legend status.

Brunch
🫧

Whipped Ricotta & Honey

Blend fresh ricotta until fluffy, spread generously, drizzle with truffle honey, and finish with cracked black pepper and a few thyme leaves. Absurdly delicious on grilled sourdough.

Elegant
🍅

Pan con Tomate

Spain's greatest breakfast — grate ripe tomatoes directly onto toasted bread, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt. The bread soaks up the tomato juice like a sponge. Perfection.

Spanish
🧄

Roasted Garlic Spread

Slow-roast a full head of garlic until golden and soft, then squeeze the caramelized cloves onto crusty bread. Mix with softened butter for compound garlic butter that'll ruin you for anything else.

Flavor Bomb
🫐

Labneh & Za'atar

Thick, tangy labneh (strained yogurt) spread on flatbread, topped with a generous sprinkle of za'atar and a glug of olive oil. A Middle Eastern classic that's impossibly addictive.

Middle Eastern
🐟

Sardines & Lemon

Quality tinned sardines (in olive oil) on crusty bread with a squeeze of lemon, thinly sliced red onion, and capers. Underrated, deeply savory, and incredibly nutritious.

Underrated
Perfect Matches
